# What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity ka matlab hota hai computers, networks, servers, mobile devices
aur data ko cyber attacks se protect karna.

Aaj ke digital world mein almost har cheez internet se connected hai,
is liye cybersecurity bohat zyada important ho chuki hai.

## Why Cybersecurity is Important

- Personal data protection (passwords, photos, bank details)
- Financial fraud se bachao
- Company aur government systems ko secure rakhna
- Cyber crimes ko prevent karna

## Cybersecurity vs Information Security

Cybersecurity:
- Focus internet aur network based systems par hota hai

Information Security:
- Focus data par hota hai (digital + physical)

## Cyber Attack vs Cyber Crime

Cyber Attack:
- System ko harm karne ki attempt

Cyber Crime:
- Jab attack illegal activity ke liye use ho

## Real World Examples

- Banking apps ka secure hona
- Social media accounts ka hacking se bachao
- Government websites ki protection

## Key Takeaways

- Cybersecurity systems aur data ko protect karti hai
- Internet ke sath risks bhi barhte ja rahe hain
- Strong security digital life ke liye zaroori hai
